# Global Strategic Marketing: Chukjeok Coffee Market Entry

## Overview
This project presents a comprehensive global marketing strategy for **Chukjeok Coffee**, an affordable and high-caffeine Ready-to-Drink (RTD) coffee brand. The strategy targets the high-pressure environment of South Korea, specifically focusing on students and "9-to-5" workers who require quick, reliable energy boosts.

## Brand Identity
- **Name**: Chukjeok Coffee (derived from the Korean word for "accumulate" or "build up," reflecting the brand's goal to power continuous activity).
- **Core Value**: Providing a strong, convenient coffee solution for the middle-to-middle-low income segment.
- **Positioning**: A functional "rush" beverage that supports productivity in a fast-paced urban culture.

## Market Analysis & Strategy
- **Target Market**: South Korea, selected for its intense coffee consumption culture and high density of convenience stores.
- **Consumer Profile**: Gen Z students and office workers facing high workloads (the "Pallipalli" culture).
- **Marketing Mix (4Ps)**:
  - **Product**: RTD coffee with high caffeine content and ergonomic packaging for on-the-go consumption.
  - **Price**: Competitive pricing aimed at the middle-low economic segment to ensure daily affordability.
  - **Place**: Heavy focus on convenience store chains (CVS) like GS25, CU, and 7-Eleven.
  - **Promotion**: Digital-first approach using social media, micro-influencers, and localized "Buy 1 Get 1" campaigns near universities and business districts.

## Business Frameworks Applied
- **Business Model Canvas (BMC)**: Mapping key partners, value propositions, and revenue streams.
- **STP Analysis**: Detailed Segmentation, Targeting, and Positioning for the East Asian market.
- **SWOT Analysis**: Identifying competitive advantages in the saturated Korean RTD market.
- **Competitor Benchmarking**: Analysis against established local and international players.
